Open Historia — The Free Pax Historia Alternative

Open Historia is a free, open-source grand strategy game — a community-driven alternative to Pax Historia — with AI-powered diplomacy, a full vector map editor, custom scenarios, troop combat, and cross-platform play. No purchase, no subscriptions, no DRM — just download and play.

Why Switch from Pax Historia?

Pax Historia is a popular commercial grand strategy game developed by Pax Historia Games. It offers a nation-commanding experience with a world map, but it requires payment to play and the development roadmap is controlled by a single company.

Open Historia was built by grand strategy fans who wanted:

  • A completely free alternative — no purchase, no microtransactions, no subscriptions
  • Open-source transparency — the full codebase is on GitHub under the MIT license
  • Community-driven development — features are shaped by players, not shareholders
  • Self-hostable servers — run your own instance, play offline, control your data
  • AI-powered diplomacy — negotiate with any nation through natural language, not fixed dialogue options
  • A powerful map editor — create and share custom scenarios with full vector editing tools

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Open Historia really free?

Yes. Open Historia is completely free under the MIT license. No purchase, no microtransactions, no subscriptions, no ads. The source code is fully open on GitHub and always will be.

Is Open Historia the same as Pax Historia?

No. Open Historia is an independent project inspired by the grand strategy genre. It is not affiliated with Pax Historia Games. It's built from scratch as a community-driven, open-source alternative for players who want a free grand strategy experience with AI-powered diplomacy and a full map editor.

Can I play Open Historia offline?

Yes. Once installed, Open Historia runs entirely offline. If you connect a local LLM (like Ollama or LM Studio), even AI features work without an internet connection. See the self-hosting guide.

Is there an Android version?

Yes. A thin client APK is available that connects to a running Open Historia server. Download it from the Android release page. The game logic runs on the server; the APK is a remote display client.

Can I create and share my own scenarios?

Yes. Use the full vector map editor to draw regions, place cities, set owners, and define starting conditions. Publish your scenario to the Scenario Hub from the in-game Community tab and share it with players worldwide.
